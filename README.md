# My Favorite Movie: Pitch Perfect

**Genre:** Comedy, Musical  
**Release Year:** 2012  
**Directed by:** Jason Moore  

## About the Movie
*Pitch Perfect* is a musical comedy film about college a cappella groups competing to win the national championship.  
It focuses on **Beca Mitchell**, a college student who joins the all-girls group **The Barden Bellas** and helps transform their sound with her creativity and love for remixing music.
